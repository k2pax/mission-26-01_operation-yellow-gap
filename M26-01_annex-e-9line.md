# Annex E to OPORD 26-01, YELLOW GAP — 9-Line Emergency/MEDEVAC Card

**Classification:** UNCLASSIFIED // PERSONAL USE ONLY

**Purpose:** The standard military 9-line MEDEVAC request, adapted for a civilian calling 911 or local SAR. Lines 6, 8, and 9 are repurposed from their wartime meaning (enemy activity, EPW status, NBC contamination) to what a civilian dispatcher actually needs. Read it in order, full and complete, per the OPORD's existing instruction not to improvise this under stress.

Fill what you can ahead of time; the rest gets filled in the moment.

---
